Chocolate Bar

A 40% split keyboard

Comes in three layouts, every one of them interchangable.

Buy

Why?

I wanted a split 40% keyboard but could not find any place that sold a row staggered version (from my 5 minutes of "research"), so I designed and built this.
It comes with three different layouts.
Since the layouts use the same number of keys and layout, they all use the same software.

The Layouts

Row Staggered

For the noobs who want to go into 40% keyboards but can't seem to figure out the ortholinear layout. (like me)

Ortholinear

This is what the keyboard was named after. I think it looks the cutest.

Column Staggered

The most ergonomic option in this series. Also using this provides an additional boost of street cred.
